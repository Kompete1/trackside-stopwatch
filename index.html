Quick Track Guide

Start Fast

Tap any Lap button once to start that driver. Tap the same Lap button again to close the lap and roll straight into the next one.

Sector Splits

Each Split button records a sector time. Split 2 compares only with earlier Split 2 times, not with Split 1.

Pause Safely

Use Menu and Stop Timing to pause every running driver without ending the current lap. Use the Lap button again to resume.

Feedback Controls

Open Settings to adjust Tap Sound, Tap Haptics, and Tap Feedback Strength to match your trackside environment.

Recover Sessions

The app saves timing locally. If the page reloads, you can restore the last session and continue where you left off. Use Export to download completed laps as CSV.
