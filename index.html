Stopwatch User Guide

Welcome
Quickly capture lap and split times for 1, 2, or 4 drivers simultaneously. Designed for clarity and ease-of-use.

Modes
Tap header to cycle modes:
- 1 Driver Mode: Detailed timing for one driver.
- 2 Driver Mode: Detailed timing for two drivers.
- 4 Driver Mode: Compact timing view for four drivers.

Buttons
- Lap (L#): Tap to start/end laps. Automatically tracks best lap times.
- Split (S#): Tap mid-lap to record split times.
- Menu Options (tap "Menu")
- Stop Timing: Pauses all timers.
- Reset Timing: Clears all timing data.
- User Guide: Shows this help guide.
- OK Button: Currently inactive (reserved for future use).

Good luck at the track!
